About Neelkanth Cables

Neelkanth Cables Ltd., incorporated in the year 2013 and positioned at Masaiti area of Ndola in the Copperbelt Province of Zambia, is one of the leading Cable Manufacturing companies in Africa.

Our company is equipped with a state-of-art cables manufacturing facility - a Greenfield project which has been set-up to manufacture all Cable types up to and including Voltage Grade of 33kV.

We are one of the largest cable manufacturers in the Southern & Central and East African Region. Over the years, we have acquired more than 50% market share in the domestic market for Low Voltage and Medium Voltage cables.

To maintain our product quality we use 99.995% pure copper from LME registered copper mines only. Our success story inspires 500+ dealers and 2000+ retailers to distribute our products on priority so that the end customer is always satisfied and happy.

Neelkanth Group Journey

Motivation to

Start Cable Manufacturing

Zambia very rich in mineral and metals and Copper is found in great abundance throughout the country. The potential to convert abundant resources is under utilized in Zambia by the manufacturing sector. Traders find it easier to import the finished product and sell it then to invest in plant and machinery and manufacture it. It is disheartening to see that a material, which is in great abundance in the country, is being resold in the market at exorbitantly higher price once it is converted into finished goods. These facts were taken into account when the group planned for setting up the manufacturing facility.
