About Neelkanth Cables

Neelkanth Cables Ltd., incorporated in the year 2013 and positioned at Masaiti area of Ndola in the Copperbelt Province of Zambia, is one of the leading Cable Manufacturing companies in Africa.

Our company is equipped with a state-of-art cables manufacturing facility - a Greenfield project which has been set-up to manufacture all Cable types up to and including Voltage Grade of 33kV.

We are one of the largest cable manufacturers in the Southern & Central and East African Region. Over the years, we have acquired more than 50% market share in the domestic market for Low Voltage and Medium Voltage cables.

What Motivated Us

To Start Cable Manufacturing

Zambia very rich in mineral and metals and Copper is found in great abundance throughout the country. The potential to convert abundant resources is under utilized in Zambia by the manufacturing sector. Traders find it easier to import the finished product and sell it then to invest in plant and machinery and manufacture it. It is disheartening to see that a material, which is in great abundance in the country, is being resold in the market at exorbitantly higher price once it is converted into finished goods. These facts were taken into account when the group planned for setting up the manufacturing facility.

Message from Board of Directors

Dear Esteemed Customers and Business Partners,

It gives us immense pleasure to introduce Neelkanth Cables Limited (NCL), as one of the leading Cable Manufacturing Companies in Africa.

The objective of Neelkanth Cables is of doing a “Value Addition” to locally mined Copper by manufacturing the best in class Low & Medium Voltage Cable products confirming to various National & International Standards at a highly competitive price and thereby serving to the discrete Market Sectors among the member countries of SADC region.

Considering the development Policy of Government of Zambia, at present the country is experiencing a rapid growth in Infrastructure Development & Electrification. This is an apt moment for the domestic manufacturers to contribute in the national development by ensuring that discrete products for the relevant sector are readily available in the Zambian Market at an affordable cost and thereby the imports can be minimized. Producing the products locally and that too in accordance with International standards will not only boost the Zambian Markets but will also open the unexplored venues for exports and this in turn will add to the fiscal consolidation of Zambia’s Economy.

Further, NCL also takes this opportunity to assure each of its Business Associates/Partners, that this association which has been established on the trust, shall be eternal and would certainly reap mutual growth & progress in the future. To achieve this, NCL seeks the blessings of all its partners for making NCL a successful venture.
